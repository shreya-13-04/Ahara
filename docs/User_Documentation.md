## 1. Introduction

### What is Ahara?

**Ahara** is a mobile application designed to tackle one of the most pressing challenges of our time — food waste. Every day, restaurants, event organizers, institutional canteens, and catering businesses prepare more food than they can serve. This surplus food, which is perfectly safe to eat, often ends up discarded.

Ahara bridges the gap between those who have surplus food and those who can benefit from it. By connecting **Sellers** (food providers), **Buyers** (individuals or organizations looking for affordable or free food), and **Volunteers** (people who help transport the food), Ahara creates a reliable, accountable, and efficient food redistribution network.

### Our Goal

- Significantly reduce avoidable food waste at the community level.
- Make surplus food accessible to buyers at low or no cost.
- Create meaningful social impact through volunteer-driven last-mile delivery.
- Build trust and accountability among all stakeholders through a transparent rating system.

### What Can You Do with Ahara?

| Role | What You Can Do |
|---|---|
| **Seller** | List surplus food, manage orders, track volunteer delivery, receive ratings |
| **Buyer** | Discover food on a map, claim or purchase listings, track delivery, rate sellers/volunteers |
| **Volunteer** | Accept delivery tasks, navigate to sellers, deliver to buyers, earn ratings |

## 2. Target Users

Ahara is built for three distinct types of users. Each has a dedicated experience within the application.

### 2.1 Seller

A **Seller** is any individual or business that regularly produces or sells food and has surplus on certain days. This includes:

- Restaurants and cafes
- Cloud kitchens
- Event management companies
- Institutional cafeterias (schools, colleges, offices)
- Catering businesses
- Pet shops with surplus animal-safe food

Sellers list their surplus food on the app with details such as quantity, food type, dietary category, and pickup time. They can offer food for free or at a discounted price.

### 2.2 Buyer

A **Buyer** is any person or organization that wants to claim or purchase surplus food. This can include:

- Individuals looking for affordable meals
- NGOs and community kitchens
- Families in need of emergency food support

Buyers discover food listings on a map, apply filters, claim what they need, and choose whether to pick it up themselves or have a volunteer deliver it.

### 2.3 Volunteer

A **Volunteer** is an individual who offers their time and transport to pick up food from a seller's location and deliver it to a buyer. Volunteers are a critical part of the Ahara ecosystem — they make last-mile delivery possible.

Volunteers can use a car, bike, cycle, or walk depending on their capacity. They must pass a verification process before accepting delivery tasks.

## 4. Account Registration and Login

Ahara uses a secure registration process that includes OTP (One-Time Password) verification via your mobile number to confirm your identity.

### 4.1 How to Open the App and Register

1. Launch the **Ahara** app on your smartphone.
2. On the welcome screen, tap **"Get Started"** or **"Register"**.
3. You will be shown a **Role Selection** screen. Choose one of the following: **Seller**, **Buyer**, or **Volunteer**.
4. You will be taken to the registration form specific to your chosen role.

---

### 4.2 Seller Registration

To register as a Seller, fill in the following details:

1. **Business Name** — Enter the name of your restaurant, cafe, or business.
2. **Business Type** — Select from the dropdown: Restaurant, Cafe, Cloud Kitchen, Event Management, Cafeteria, or Pet Shop.
3. **FSSAI Number** — Enter your 14-digit Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) licence number. This is mandatory for food safety compliance.
4. **Contact Number** — Enter your 10-digit mobile number and tap **"Verify Phone with OTP"**.
   - A 6-digit OTP will be sent to your number by SMS.
   - Enter the OTP on the verification screen.
   - Once verified, you will see a green **"Phone Verified"** confirmation.
5. **Business Email** — Enter your business email address (optional but recommended).
6. **Location** — Tap the location field to open the map picker. Navigate to your business address on the map and pin it precisely. Tap **"Confirm Location"**.
7. **Password** — Set a strong password (minimum 8 characters).
8. Tap **"Create Seller Account"** to complete registration.
9. You will be redirected to the **Login** page. Log in using your registered email and password.

---

### 4.3 Buyer Registration

To register as a Buyer, fill in the following details:

1. **Full Name** — Enter your full name.
2. **Phone Number** — Enter your 10-digit mobile number and tap **"Verify Phone with OTP"**.
   - Enter the OTP received via SMS to verify your number.
3. **Email Address** — Enter a valid email address.
4. **Password** — Set a password with a minimum of 6 characters.
5. **Location** — Tap the location icon to detect your current location automatically, or search for your area manually.
6. Tap **"Create Account"** to complete registration.
7. You will be redirected to the **Login** page.

---

### 4.4 Volunteer Registration

Volunteer registration includes an additional identity verification step to ensure the safety of all parties involved.

1. **Full Name** — Enter your full name.
2. **Mode of Transport** — Select from: Car, Bike, Cycle, or Walk.
3. **Date of Birth** *(required only for Car and Bike users)* — Select your date of birth from the calendar. You must be at least **18 years old** to volunteer with a motorized vehicle.
4. **Email Address** — Enter your email address.
5. **Contact Number** — Enter your mobile number and verify using OTP, as described above.
6. **Location** — Select your current location using the map picker.
7. **Aadhaar Identity Verification** — Tap **"Start"** next to the Aadhaar verification panel.
   - You will be asked to enter your Aadhaar details.
   - Once verified, the panel will display **"Aadhaar e-KYC Verified"** in green.
8. **Password** — Set a password with a minimum of 6 characters.
9. Tap **"Create Volunteer Account"** to submit your registration.
10. Log in using your credentials.

---

### 4.5 Login

1. Open the Ahara app and tap **"Login"**.
2. Enter your registered **Email Address** and **Password**.
3. Tap **"Login"**. You will be directed to your role-specific home screen.

> If you forget your password, use the **"Forgot Password?"** option on the login screen to reset it via your registered email.

## 5. Seller User Guide

Once logged in as a Seller, you will be taken to your **Seller Dashboard**, which provides a complete overview of your listings, incoming orders, and performance.

---

### 5.1 Navigating the Seller Dashboard

The Seller Dashboard includes the following key sections:

- **Overview** — A summary of your active listings, total orders, and income.
- **My Listings** — Manage all your active and past food listings.
- **Orders** — View and manage incoming orders from buyers.
- **Notifications** — Alerts for new orders and status updates.
- **Profile** — Manage your business profile and account settings.

---

### 5.2 Creating a Food Listing

A Listing is how you advertise your surplus food to buyers. Follow these steps:

1. From the Dashboard, tap **"Create Listing"** (the prominent button on your home screen).
2. **Food Image** — Optionally, tap the image area to upload a photo of the food from your camera or gallery. A preview image may also be auto-generated based on the food name.
3. **Business Information** — Select your **Type of Business** from the dropdown (e.g., Restaurant, Cafeteria).
4. **Food Name** — Enter a descriptive name, e.g., *"Mixed Veg Biryani"* or *"Assorted Sandwiches"*.
5. **Food Type** — Select the appropriate category (e.g., Prepared Meal, Packaged Food, etc.).
6. **Food Diet** — Select dietary type: **Veg**, **Non-Veg**, **Vegan**, or **Jain**.
7. **Quantity** — Enter the quantity available and select the unit: kg, portions, pieces, or litres.
8. **Redistribution Mode** — Choose:
   - **Free** — The food is offered at no cost.
   - **Discounted** — Enter a reduced price in Indian Rupees (₹).
9. **Preparation Time** — Select the date and time the food was prepared.
10. **Rescue Window (Optional)** — Enable a specific pickup window (e.g., 10:00 PM – 11:00 PM). The window cannot exceed 4 hours and must fall within the food's safe consumption window.
11. **Hygiene Status** — Select your cleanliness assessment: Excellent, Good, or Fair.
12. **Description** — Add any relevant notes such as allergens, portion sizes, or special instructions.
13. **Pickup Location** — Tap the map area to open the map picker and pin your exact pickup location. Enter your pincode if prompted.
14. Tap **"Create Listing"** to publish your listing.

Your listing will appear on the buyer-facing map immediately.

---

### 5.3 Managing Your Listings

1. Tap **"My Listings"** from the bottom navigation or dashboard.
2. You will see a list of all your current and past listings, each showing:
   - Food name and image
   - Quantity remaining
   - Status: **Active**, **Claimed**, or **Expired**
3. To **edit** a listing, tap the listing card and select **"Edit"**. Modify the required fields and tap **"Update Listing"**.
4. To **cancel/remove** a listing, tap the listing and select **"Cancel Listing"**. Confirm the action. The listing will be removed from the buyer map.

---

### 5.4 Viewing Orders

1. Tap **"Orders"** from the navigation menu.
2. You will see a list of all orders placed against your listings.
3. Each order card shows:
   - Buyer information
   - Food item and quantity claimed
   - Delivery method: Self-Pickup or Volunteer Delivery
   - Current order status (e.g., Confirmed, Volunteer Assigned, Picked Up, Delivered)
4. Tap an order card to view its **full details**, including buyer address (for volunteer deliveries) and a real-time status timeline.

---

### 5.5 Tracking Volunteer Delivery

When a buyer selects volunteer delivery for your order:

1. Go to **"Orders"** and tap the relevant order.
2. Once a volunteer accepts the task, the order status changes to **"Volunteer Assigned"**.
3. The order detail page will display the volunteer's name and transport mode.
4. As the delivery progresses, the status will update to **"Picked Up"**, **"In Transit"**, and finally **"Delivered"**.

---

### 5.6 Receiving Ratings

After an order is delivered, buyers may rate your listing and service. These ratings:
- Appear on your seller profile as a star rating.
- Contribute to your overall **Seller Trust Score**, which is displayed to buyers when they view your listings.
- Help you attract more buyers over time.

You can view all your ratings and reviews by navigating to your **Profile** and selecting **"Ratings & Reviews"**.

---

### 5.7 Getting Your Business Verified

Sellers can get their profile verified with an official badge to increase buyer confidence:

1. Navigate to **Profile → Business Verification**.
2. Upload relevant documents (e.g., FSSAI certificate, business registration).
3. Once reviewed and approved, your profile will display a **Verified Seller** badge.

## 6. Buyer User Guide

As a Buyer, you can discover surplus food near you, claim it, and either pick it up yourself or have it delivered by a volunteer.

---

### 6.1 Navigating the Buyer Home Screen

The Buyer home screen includes:

- **Browse (Map View)** — Discover listings on an interactive map.
- **Orders** — View your active and past order history.
- **Favourites** — Access your saved favourite restaurants/sellers.
- **Profile** — Manage your account, address, and preferences.

---

### 6.2 Discovering Food Listings on the Map

1. Open the app and tap the **"Browse"** tab.
2. An interactive map will load, centered on your current location.
3. Food listings appear as **price tags** (e.g., *"FREE"* or *"₹30"*) pinned on the map.
4. Tap a pin to see a quick preview of the listing (food name, quantity, and distance).
5. Tap the preview card or **"View Details"** to see the full listing page.
6. Use the **blue dot** on the map to identify your current location. Tap the **locate me** button (bottom right) to re-center the map on your position.
7. If no listings are visible in the current area, drag the map to a different area and tap **"Search in this area"** when it appears.

---

### 6.3 Applying Filters

To narrow down results:

1. Tap the **"Filter"** button at the top of the browse screen.
2. You can filter by:
   - **Rating** — Only show listings from sellers with a minimum star rating.
   - **Free Only** — Show only listings offered at no cost.
3. Tap **"Apply"** to confirm your filters. Active filters appear as chips below the search bar.
4. To reset filters, tap the **"✕"** icon next to the filter chips.

> **Note:** Ahara also automatically applies your **dietary preferences** (set in your profile) to filter results. For example, if you are vegetarian, non-vegetarian listings will not appear.

---

### 6.4 Claiming or Purchasing a Listing

1. Open a listing's detail page.
2. Review the following information:
   - Food name, photo, and dietary type (Veg / Non-Veg / Vegan / Jain)
   - Quantity available, price (Free or discounted)
   - Pickup window (the time window during which the food must be collected)
   - Seller name and rating
   - Hygiene status and description
3. Tap **"Claim"** or **"Order Now"** to proceed.
4. You will be taken to the **Checkout** page.

---

### 6.5 Selecting Pickup or Volunteer Delivery

On the Checkout page:

1. Choose your **delivery method**:
   - **Self Pickup** — You will go to the seller's location and collect the food yourself. The seller's address (as pinned on the map) will be displayed.
   - **Volunteer Delivery** — A verified Ahara volunteer will pick up the food from the seller and deliver it to your address.
2. If you choose **Volunteer Delivery**, confirm or enter your delivery address.
3. Review the order summary and tap **"Confirm Order"** or proceed to **Payment** (for discounted listings).
4. For paid orders, complete the payment via the integrated payment gateway.

Your order is now placed and confirmed.

---

### 6.6 Tracking Your Order

1. Go to **"Orders"** from the bottom navigation.
2. Tap your active order to view its status.
3. The order status page shows a **real-time timeline**:
   - ✅ Order Placed
   - ✅ Seller Confirmed
   - ✅ Volunteer Assigned *(if volunteer delivery)*
   - ✅ Picked Up from Seller
   - ✅ In Transit
   - ✅ Delivered
4. You will receive in-app notifications at key status changes.

---

### 6.7 Rating Sellers and Volunteers

After your order is delivered:

1. Open the completed order from **"Orders"**.
2. Tap **"Rate & Review"**.
3. Rate the **Seller** on a scale of 1 to 5 stars and leave an optional comment.
4. If a volunteer delivered your order, you can also rate the **Volunteer** separately.
5. Tap **"Submit Review"** to publish your rating.

Your ratings help others make informed decisions and reward dedicated sellers and volunteers.

---

### 6.8 Saving Favourite Restaurants

1. Open a seller's listing or profile.
2. Tap the **heart icon** (♡) to save them to your Favourites.
3. Access your saved sellers by tapping **"Favourites"** in the navigation bar.
4. To remove a seller from favourites, tap the filled heart icon (❤️) on their profile or in the Favourites list.

---

### 6.9 Setting Your Dietary Preferences

1. Go to **Profile → Account Details**.
2. Under **Dietary Preferences**, select the options that apply to you (Vegetarian, Non-Vegetarian, Vegan, Jain).
3. Tap **"Save"**. The browse feed will now be automatically filtered based on your choices.

## 7. Volunteer User Guide

Volunteers are the heart of Ahara's delivery network. As a volunteer, you pick up food from sellers and deliver it to buyers, earning ratings and achievements along the way.

---

### 7.1 Navigating the Volunteer Home Screen

The Volunteer home screen includes:

- **Home** — Your availability status, delivery statistics, badges, and incoming rescue requests.
- **Orders** — All your active, pending, and completed delivery orders.
- **Ratings** — Your rating history and performance summary.
- **Notifications** — Real-time delivery requests and status alerts.
- **Profile** — Manage your personal information and verification documents.

---

### 7.2 Volunteer Registration (Recap)

See [Section 4.4](#44-volunteer-registration) for the full registration steps. Key requirements for volunteers are:

- Valid OTP-verified mobile number.
- Aadhaar (e-KYC) identity verification.
- Must be **18 years or older** to use a motorized vehicle (Car/Bike).

---

### 7.3 Completing Verification

After initial registration, ensure the following verifications are complete before accepting orders:

1. **Phone OTP Verification** — Done during registration.
2. **Aadhaar e-KYC** — Done during registration via the verification flow.
3. **Email Verification** *(if required)* — Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link.

Once all verifications are complete, your profile will display verification badges, and you will be eligible to receive rescue request notifications.

---

### 7.4 Setting Your Availability Status

You can toggle yourself online or offline at any time:

1. On the **Home** screen, look at the top-right area for the **availability toggle switch**.
2. Toggle it **ON (green)** to appear as available and start receiving delivery requests.
3. Toggle it **OFF (grey)** to stop receiving new requests. You will enter an **"Away"** state.

> **Important:** You will only receive incoming order requests when your availability is set to **Online**.

---

### 7.5 Accepting Orders (Rescue Requests)

When you are online and a delivery request is available near you:

1. An **alert banner** will appear at the top of your Home screen saying **"New Requests Nearby"**.
2. Scroll down to the **"Incoming Requests"** section to see the request card(s).
3. Each request card shows:
   - The food item to be picked up
   - The seller's location
   - A brief message about the delivery
4. Tap **"Accept"** on a request card to take on the delivery task.
5. The order will now appear in your **"Orders"** tab with status **"Volunteer Accepted"**.

---

### 7.6 Navigating to the Pickup Location

1. Go to **"Orders"** and tap your active delivery order.
2. The order detail page will show the **Seller's pickup address** and a **"Navigate"** button.
3. Tap **"Navigate"** to open the directions in your preferred maps application.
4. Travel to the seller's location and collect the food as agreed.
5. Once you have the food, go back to the order detail page and tap **"Confirm Pickup"** to update the status to **"Picked Up"**.

---

### 7.7 Delivering the Food to the Buyer

1. After confirming pickup, the Buyer's delivery address will be displayed on the order detail page.
2. Tap **"Navigate"** to get directions to the buyer's location.
3. Deliver the food to the buyer.
4. Once delivered, tap **"Confirm Delivery"** on the order detail page.
5. The order status will update to **"Delivered"**, and the buyer will be notified.

---

### 7.8 Managing Order Status

At any point during a delivery, you can view and update the status of your order:

| Status | What It Means |
|---|---|
| Volunteer Assigned | You have been matched to an order |
| Volunteer Accepted | You accepted the request |
| Picked Up | You collected the food from the seller |
| In Transit | You are on the way to the buyer |
| Delivered | The food has been successfully delivered |

Update the status as each step is completed using the buttons on the Order Detail page.

---

### 7.9 Receiving Ratings

After each successful delivery:

- The **Buyer** may rate you on a scale of 1 to 5 stars.
- Ratings are visible on your **Volunteer Profile**.
- Your **average rating** is shown on your home screen dashboard.
- High ratings earn you **achievement badges** such as "Top Star" (4.5+ average) and "Social Hero" (5+ deliveries).

## 9. Ratings and Trust System

Ahara uses a transparent, community-driven rating system to build trust between all users.

### 9.1 How Ratings Work

- After each completed order, **Buyers** can rate both the **Seller** (for food quality and accuracy) and the **Volunteer** (for timeliness and professionalism).
- Ratings are given on a scale of **1 star (Poor) to 5 stars (Excellent)**.
- Buyers can also leave a **written review** to provide more detailed feedback.
- All submitted ratings are immediately visible on the respective Seller or Volunteer profile.

### 9.2 Seller Ratings

- A seller's **average star rating** is shown on every listing card that appears on the buyer's map.
- Buyers can tap on a seller's profile to read all reviews before claiming food.
- Sellers with a consistently high rating are ranked more prominently in search results.

### 9.3 Volunteer Ratings

- Each volunteer's **average rating** is displayed on their profile page.
- High ratings unlock **achievement badges** (e.g., Top Star, 20+ Club, Social Hero).
- Volunteers with consistently low ratings may be reviewed by Ahara administrators.

### 9.4 Fair Review Policy

- A buyer can only rate a seller or volunteer **once per order**.
- Ratings are tied to verified, completed orders — this prevents false or fake reviews.

## 10. Safety and Verification

Ahara takes the safety of food, users, and communities very seriously. Multiple verification and trust mechanisms are in place.

### 10.1 Seller Verification

- Sellers are required to provide their **FSSAI licence number** during registration. This ensures they are legally authorized to handle and sell food.
- Sellers can optionally upload business verification documents for a **"Verified Seller"** badge.
- Listings include a mandatory **Hygiene Status** field, which helps buyers understand the cleanliness standards of the food being offered.

### 10.2 Volunteer Verification

Volunteer verification is progressive and multi-layered:

1. **Phone OTP Verification** — Confirms ownership of a valid mobile number.
2. **Aadhaar e-KYC Verification** — Confirms the volunteer's real identity using the government-issued Aadhaar card.
3. **Age Restriction** — Volunteers who select Car or Bike as their transport mode must be at least **18 years old**, verified through their date of birth during registration.
4. **Email Verification** — An optional additional layer of account security.

Only fully verified volunteers are permitted to accept and complete delivery orders.

### 10.3 Food Safety Controls

- Each listing includes a **Preparation Time**, which is used to automatically calculate a safe consumption window depending on the food type.
- Listings with an expired pickup window are automatically removed from the buyer's map.
- The **Rescue Window** feature ensures that food offered during late-night hours can only be claimed within a safe, defined time slot (maximum 4-hour window).

### 10.4 Trust History

- All activity on Ahara — including orders, ratings, and verifications — is logged in a **Trust History** associated with each user's account.
- This history can be reviewed by Ahara administrators to identify and address any misuse or fraudulent activity.

## 11. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q1. I am a seller. What happens if I prepare food but no volunteers are available for delivery?**

> Buyers always have the option to choose **Self Pickup**. If no volunteers are available, buyers will need to collect the food from your pinned location directly. You will still be notified of claimed orders, and buyers can refer to your pinned address on the map for navigation.

---

**Q2. How do I cancel an order as a buyer?**

> Open the **"Orders"** tab and select the order you wish to cancel. Tap the **"Cancel Order"** option on the order detail page. Note that cancellation may only be possible before a volunteer has been assigned. Once a volunteer is en route, cancellation is restricted to avoid wasting the volunteer's effort.

---

**Q3. As a volunteer, what if I cannot complete a delivery after accepting it?**

> Open the active order in the **"Orders"** tab and tap **"Unable to Deliver"** or contact Ahara support through the Profile menu. The system will attempt to re-assign the order to another available volunteer. Repeated cancellations after acceptance may affect your volunteer rating and account standing.

---

**Q4. Is my Aadhaar or personal information stored securely?**

> Yes. Ahara uses encrypted data storage and does not share your personal identification details with third parties. Aadhaar information is used solely for the purpose of identity verification. Refer to Ahara's Privacy Policy for full details.

---

**Q5. What should I do if I receive incorrect or spoiled food?**

> After delivery, you will be prompted to rate the seller. Use the written review option to describe the issue. You can also raise a formal complaint by contacting Ahara support through the app's **Profile → Help & Support** section. Listings that consistently receive complaints are flagged for admin review.

---

**Q6. Can I use Ahara without a smartphone?**

> Currently, Ahara is available as a mobile application for Android and iOS smartphones only. A web version is not available at this time.

---

**Q7. I am a seller. Does Ahara support multiple languages?**

> During registration, you can set your preferred **language**. The Ahara app supports multiple regional languages. Go to **Profile → Language Preferences** to change your display language.

---

**Q8. As a buyer, how do I know the food is safe to eat?**

> Each listing displays the following safety information:
> - The **preparation time** (when the food was cooked)
> - The **remaining pickup window** (auto-calculated based on food type)
> - The seller's **hygiene status** (Excellent / Good / Fair)
> - The seller's **overall rating and reviews** from past buyers
>
> We strongly recommend reviewing this information before claiming any listing.
